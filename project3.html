Build an app

The third project in our Multimediadesign career was to come up with a design and a prototype for my own app.

This was a very interesting project. We had to chose a theme for the app, Weather app, Listings App or a Sportstracking app. I chose the Weather app theme and specified it to outdoor people, especially for people who love to go fishing. So I designed a layout and a colour scheme for the app. I also made all the icons in the app and I am very pleased with the final result.

Reflection of learnings

What I have learned

This project had me venturing down the graphic design route. I learned very much, for example how to make icons, how to design specifically for smartphones and tablets and I also learned about the thought process behind a layout and colours.

What I expected to learn

What I learned in this process was exactly what I expected. I had an expectation for me to learn about graphic design, visual tools and methods and development methods, along with user tests. I am very pleased with both the finished product and learnings in this project.

What more do I need to learn

One can always learn more and I try to learn as much as possible about different designs and processes, because that particular subject is very exciting and interresting.
